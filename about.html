About Umang

Our vision: A society where survivors of acid attacks receive timely care, justice, and lasting opportunities.

History & approach

Umang was founded (imaginary organization for a class project) to raise awareness and offer practical support to survivors. The initiative focuses on connecting individuals to medical, legal, and emotional support networks through a compassionate, community-based approach.

About the creator

This website was developed by Khushi (100166915) as part of a student project at Capilano University. The goal is to demonstrate accessible, ethical web design and social impact communication.
