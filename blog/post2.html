Survivor Spotlight: Rekha's Story

September 20, 2025

Rekha survived an attack two years ago. With medical support and peer counselling, she completed a tailoring course and now runs a small home-based tailoring service. Her resilience inspires our community. Read her full story and the steps that helped her rebuild.

Resources used

  1. Immediate medical attention and referral
  2. Legal aid for case filing
  3. Vocational training and microgrant
