Community Awareness Drive: Free Medical Camp

October 1, 2025

Last weekend, Umang partnered with local clinics to host a free medical evaluation camp for survivors and at-risk women. We provided wound care referrals, counselling sign-ups, and vocational training sign-ups. Thank you to all volunteers.

How to help

  • Volunteer for our next camp
  • Donate supplies (bandages, sunscreen, prosthetic supplies)
  • Share our blog to raise awareness
