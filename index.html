Welcome to Umang

Umang supports survivors of acid attacks through medical navigation, legal support, counselling, vocational training and community awareness programs. We advocate for survivors' rights and long-term reintegration.

What we do:

  • Medical referrals & rehabilitation assistance
  • Legal aid and advocacy
  • Psycho-social counselling
  • Skills training and job placement
